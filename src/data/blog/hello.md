This is the canonical home for my long-form writing on AI engineering.

## What you'll find here

Posts on the parts of AI that show up after the model is "done" — integration, evaluation, observability, agent design, RAG systems that survive contact with real enterprise data. The unsexy 80%.

I publish here first. Long-form pieces get syndicated to Medium, but the canonical version always lives on this domain.

## Where else I show up

- Short-form takes: [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/evgenirusev/)
- Code and demos: [GitHub](https://github.com/evgenirusev)
- Syndicated long-form: [Medium](https://medium.com/@evgenirusev)

## What I'll cover

A non-exhaustive list of what's coming:

- **Agents** — when to use them, when not to, and what makes them production-ready
- **RAG** — hybrid search, evaluation, the failure modes nobody writes about
- **Evals** — how to actually measure if your AI works, vs vibes-based "looks good"
- **Production AI** — the integration, governance, and ops layers that make pilots ship
- **Field notes** — patterns I see across industries (manufacturing, mining, finance)
